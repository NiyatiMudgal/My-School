The School

Education is a social vehicle, which must forge ahead and be a source of new ideas. The school thus endeavours to provide the right opportunities and environment for the harmonious development of the personality of the student by giving a thorough and general education and also conserving the cultural heritage of the country. The School Emblem depicts "GODDESS SARASWATI" the Goddess of Wisdom and Knowledge . Our motto "Wisdom Is Glory" clearly highlights the mission of our school, i.e. to spread the light of Wisdom. We believe in developing the character, confidence and creativity of students, so that they are equipped to deal with any situation they come across. As such, we are deeply committed to the teaching learning process, which is a part of every school. Our mission is to nurture each child, just as a sapling is nurtured to enable it to grow into a strong and healthy tree. With the right foundation, we are sure that our students will grow up to be confident young individuals, the truly global citizens of tomorrow.
